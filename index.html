Departments

The ESA (Technical Students Association) is a dynamic, student-run organization that brings together engineering students from diverse disciplines. United by a passion for innovation and collaboration, ESA members represent a wide range of engineering fields, fostering interdisciplinary learning and teamwork to tackle real-world challenges.

Electrical Engineering

This department focuses on electronics, circuits, signal processing, telecommunications, and embedded systems.

Mechanical Engineering

This department deals with manufacturing processes, machine design, production planning, and quality control.

Chemical Engineering

This department deals with manufacturing processes, machine design, production planning, and quality control.

Telecommunication Engineering

This field specializes in telecommunication systems, including network design, data transmission, satellite communication, and wireless technologies.

Manufacturing Industrial and Textile Engineering

This department combines manufacturing processes, industrial systems, and textile technology.

Civil Engineering

This field focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as buildings, bridges, dams, and roads.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ESA?

ESA is a non-political, non-profit, student-run, independent, educational association. It draws its membership from students and graduates of Moi University, School of Engineering. Other persons who share in the objectives of the Association may also become members.
